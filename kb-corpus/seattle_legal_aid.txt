[CHUNK legalaid-07: How to use this directory safely]
All six organizations above are nonprofit legal-aid providers — not "notarios" and not
paid consultants. Only a licensed attorney or a Department of Justice accredited
representative can give immigration legal advice. When you call any organization:
have your document nearby, ask whether the consultation is free, ask whether they
have a Spanish-speaking staff member or interpreter, and ask how soon they can see
you. If one organization cannot take your case, ask them to refer you to another. Do
not pay anyone who guarantees a result, demands cash or gift cards, or tells you not
to read what you sign. If you have a court hearing date, mention it on the call so the
organization understands the urgency.
